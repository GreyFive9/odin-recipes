Harvest Steak Salad

Dish Info


This steak salad with is hearty, seasonal and packed with flavour. Peppery arugula is mixed with juicy grapes, shallots, and a delicious homemade balsamic vinaigrette. Served over squash and topped with candied pecans and goat cheese. Best served warm so prepare everything in advance and toss right before serving.

Ingredients


    For the Candied Pecans:

  • 1 cup unsalted pecans
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 ts flakey sea salt

    • For the Balsamic Vinaigrette:

  • 1 tbs balsamic vinegar
  • 1 ts honey
  • 1 ts Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 ts kosher salt
  • 1/8 ts fresh black pepper
  • 3 tbs extra virgin olive oil

    • For the Harvest Salad

  • 1 medium acorn squash seeded and sliced into 2-inch wedges
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 3 tbs extra virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt and fresh black pepper
  • 10 ounce steak such as NY Strip, ribeye, tenderloin, or top sirloin
  • 2 ts avocado oil
  • 5 ounces Arugula
  • 1 medium shallot thinly sliced
  • 1 cup red grapes sliced in half
  • 3.5 ounces soft goat cheese or feta

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add penne and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  3. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat and cook garlic for 1 minute. Whisk in flour until a paste forms.
  4. Pour in milk and cream, whisking continuously until smooth.
  5. Stir in 3/4 of the Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese and nutmeg. Add drained penne pasta and cooked chicken; stir to combine. Pour mixture into a casserole dish. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted, 10 to 12 minutes.
  7. Enjoy the meal!
